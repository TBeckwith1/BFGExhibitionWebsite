Welcome to The BFG in Pictures official website.

This exhibition is to show artworks from the BFG created by Quentin Blake and the exhibition will be held at The Gala Theatre in Durham.

For more information about The BFG in Pictures exhibition go to the About us section which will give you more information including dates, relevant websites and more general information about the exhibition.

Quentin Blake's BFG artworks can be found on the Artwork page in which extra detail about each piece of artwork will be accompanying each artwork piece.

To contribute to the exhibition you can submit your own dream you may have had by filling out the form on the Submit a dream page.

Another event that is happening at the Gala Durham Theatre is an event being held by illustrator Liz Million. For more information go to the Workshop page where there will be more details.

If you want to get in touch with us there are contact details and location details of where the Gala Durham Theatre is on the Contact us page.
