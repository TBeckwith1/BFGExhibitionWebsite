Details About The Exhibition

The BFG in Pictures exhibition is a 71 day event held at The Gala Gallery in Durham from 16 April - 25 June 2022.

The artwork shown at the exhibition are previously unseen Quentin Blake illustrations for Roald Dahl's The BFG book that weren't inevitably included in the final version of the BFG.
